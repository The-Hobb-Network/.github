# EssentialsRelay Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: November 2025*

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how EssentialsRelay ("the Bot"), developed by The Hobb Network, collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot service. EssentialsRelay is a bot designed to relay messages between Minecraft servers and Discord channels.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use EssentialsRelay, we may collect:
- **Server Information**: Discord server ID, channel IDs where the bot is configured
- **Message Content**: Messages relayed between Minecraft and Discord (processed in real-time, not stored long-term)
- **User Identifiers**: Discord user IDs and Minecraft usernames for message attribution

### 2.2 Configuration Data

We store configuration settings you provide, such as:
- Linked channel configurations
- Bot settings and preferences for your server

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:
- Relay messages between Minecraft and Discord
- Provide and maintain the bot's functionality
- Improve and optimize our services
- Respond to support requests

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Message content is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored
- Configuration data is securely stored and protected
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
- Use your data for purposes unrelated to the bot's functionality

## 6. Data Retention

- **Messages**: Processed in real-time, not stored permanently
- **Configuration Data**: Retained while the bot is active on your server
- **Server Data**: Deleted upon bot removal from your server (upon request)

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Request information about the data we store
- Request deletion of your server's configuration data
- Remove the bot from your server at any time

## 8. Children's Privacy

EssentialsRelay is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through our Discord server or other appropriate means.
